Music is the thing I care most about outside of work. Mostly rock and metal, though that holds less every year. Live shows are the best part — there's not much I'd rather do with a night.

I started on drums around ten and took lessons for a while, but I liked playing a lot more than practicing. At sixteen I bought a cheap acoustic guitar and didn't bother tuning it for the first week. I've been playing ever since, happily and not especially well.

Right now I'm working through <em>1001 Albums You Must Hear Before You Die</em> — the whole list, in order. It's pulled me well outside what I'd pick on my own, into jazz, soul, hip-hop, and a lot of records I'd never have found otherwise. Some of them I don't like at all, which turns out to be most of the point.
