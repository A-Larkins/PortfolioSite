I’ve always been a big reader, and I’m also a huge fan of audiobooks. Sci-fi and fantasy are my go-to genres, but I like balancing them out with nonfiction too. My favorite book is East of Eden, and I also love classics like Sherlock Holmes. I’m a big fan of A Song of Ice and Fire and Harry Potter.

My current long-term project is reading every Stephen King and John Grisham book. Two very different writers, and reading them in bulk teaches you something about craft that reading one of each never would — King builds a world by accumulating small ordinary details until the strange thing arrives and feels inevitable, while Grisham is ruthless about structure and pace, with never a wasted chapter. Watching each of them do the same thing well across dozens of books is its own kind of education.

I like to cook—mostly simple stuff like stews and easy meals. I’m also into movies, TV shows, video games, and chess whenever I can find the time.

Someday, I’d love to write a book, even though I don’t quite know what it would be about yet.
