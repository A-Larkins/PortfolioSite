I work on the Weblog application in support of NAVSEA Code 423 in Philadelphia for the U.S. Navy. Weblog is a large-scale enterprise system that consolidated six+ legacy applications into a single modern platform used by thousands of Navy personnel.

I’m an integral member of the MGTIS (Marine Gas Turbine Information System) team and was heavily involved in the transition from the legacy systems to the new ASP.NET Core Weblog application, which successfully went live on March 1, 2024. The system handles mission-critical data and operates within a secure environment, and I hold a Top Secret clearance.

My role spans the full stack, working primarily with C#, JavaScript, Entity Framework Core, and Telerik UI. I contribute to system architecture, feature development, and ongoing maintenance, working in an Agile environment and using Jira to manage and prioritize work.
