Disc golf has become the thing I look forward to all week. It started as an excuse to get outside with friends and turned into a genuine obsession — reading course layouts, working on my form, figuring out which disc does what in wind. It scratches the same itch programming does: a long feedback loop of small adjustments that eventually add up to something that works.

Before that, I grew up playing baseball, basketball, track, and soccer. These days I also lift weights regularly and play regular golf when I can.

I'm a lifelong Philadelphia sports fan — baseball and football above all, though I'll watch most things. Growing up around Philly teams is excellent preparation for a career in software: you learn early that setbacks are part of the process, and you show up again the next season anyway.
