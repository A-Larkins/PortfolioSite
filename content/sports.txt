Disc golf took over. It started as a reason to get outside with friends and turned into the thing I plan my weekends around — reading course layouts, working on my form, learning which disc does what in the wind.

Before that it was baseball, basketball, track, and soccer growing up. These days I lift regularly and still play regular golf when I get the chance.

And I'm a Philadelphia sports fan, which is less a hobby than a condition. Baseball and football above all, but I'll watch nearly anything.
